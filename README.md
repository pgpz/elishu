# elishu
**One-million-years ago, in the Andromeda Galaxy, a bright-rather small red star named, "Murak" was formed. A rocky, purplish colored temperate marine terra planet named Elishu was birthed, while also birthing a race named Elishians. Mega-annum has passed, and the Elishians now co-exist with the humans of Terra.**


current platforms: Roblox(for the starting project to decide where to go and what to do in future), Linux(for terminal games and specials), anything that can use a CPU if its writing. i will not discriminate on your OS, also. dont worry ill take the time to make stuff work on windows/mac if its a terminal game when i gain the time.


This is a fictional universe that I am crafting, which will consist of Roblox games, terminal-based games and much more when able to. It has a lot to do with astroscience, quantum physics, physics, real equation-dealt ideas(meaning, if something is not entirely fictional in the story, it WILL use real math to make it fully accurate to real life), and real thought-out emotions. The story will also include lots of tech-related things. You will see characters that can program, Linux will be the one and only operating system in the universe of Elishu!!!! One of the main storyarc characters use Gentoo Linux.

This work includes characters that of which are my friends and family and similar. If you see a character, they are most likely real or very-well inspired by an existing character from another story. Nothing at all will ever be stolen from another authors art/creation ever. 

If you wish to support this, please just share my creation. Donations are obviously accepted, but all I ask for is for you to share my creation with the world as this is my dream project I have had in my mind since I was 4 years old.

The games and everything will be open source fully, as this is a passion project, cooked up from my childhood and life.

If you wish to give suggestions, ideas, and more; my discord is @0pgp , I am open to any and all suggestions/ideas/feedback. 

This universe and series comes straight from my thoughts and mind, this is a fully anti-ai project. I am fully accepting help or assistance in making things for the project. I would love if this became a community-project storylore with the help of supporters. Will have to review them aswell, obviously though.
